What is Picotte?

What is Picotte?#

Picotte is a supercomputing cluster or high-performance computing (HPC) cluster: a set of powerful computers that are connected to each other and accessible via a uniform interface.

Picotte is built and maintained by the University Research Computing Facility, and located in the URCF server room in Curtis hall.
